Quadrotor Project Information

I designed this remote from the ground up after getting fed up with the limitations and hacky design of my previous remote.

The design is based around an Arduino Mega with an LCD touchscreen shield. A custom made PCB makes use of the extra pins on the Arduino Mega to transmit and receive data via Xbee, display information on the LCD screen and 2 tri-color LEDs, and get user inputs via motion controls or an LCD touchscreen, 12 buttons, a joystick and a sliding potentiometer.

It is controlled by my Smart Remote (another one of my projects).
