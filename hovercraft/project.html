I designed and built a small remote controlled hovercraft at the end of the 2013 summer.

The final version uses 2 electric ducted fans for thrust both with the ability to turn left/right/180Â°. This allows the hovercraft to go forwards and steer left and right but as well go backwards.

A 3rd ducted fan is built right into the body of the hovercraft and allows the skirt to fill with air and allow the hovercraft to glide on flat surfaces and float on water. This fan's speed can be adjusted to inflate/deflate the hovercraft's skirt.

It is controlled by my RF Playstation remote (another one of my projects).
