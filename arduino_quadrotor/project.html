Quadrotor Project Information

I built this quadrotor in my spare time during my final year at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

The Arduino Mega 2560 board in the center reads data from a GY-80 IMU (Internal Measurement Unit). This provides it with accelerometer and gyroscope data which it can use to calculate its pitch and roll. The program then uses a PID algorithm to determine what the motor speeds should be to keep the pitch and roll at the desired values.

It is controlled by my Smart Remote (another one of my projects).
